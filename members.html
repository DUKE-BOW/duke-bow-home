MEMBER SPOTLIGHT:

If you know a BOW member who's done something noteworthy or you're writing for The Chronicle or Medium (or any other platforms) and you have an article that you’d like to share with BOW and beyond, please e-mail your piece/link to lz109@duke.edu to be featured on our social media or in our newsletter.

BOW SCHOLARSHIP FUND:

Interested in attending a conference or other event? Apply to BOW’s scholarship fund here! BOW will fund up to $250, and you are eligible for no more than one scholarship per semester. Applications must be submitted at least 2 weeks prior to the event date.

BOW ATTENDANCE:

Freshmen, sophomores, and juniors must attend all mandatory events plus 2 optional events. Seniors must attend all mandatory events. Track your monthly attendance here.

BOW ALUMNI SHADOWING

Want to know what it is like to work at a company like Google, Accenture, or Deutsche Bank? Apply for BOW's Alumni Shadowing program to accompany an alum at her workplace in mid-late May. Explore the shadowing opportunities via this document , and if you are interested, fill out this brief application by Wednesday, April 3 at midnight!

BOW CLOTHING CLOSET:

In order to make business attire more accessible to everyone, BOW's Diversity & Inclusion Committee has launched a clothing closet. This resource contains a contact list of BOW members willing to short-term lend their business-formal and business-casual clothing for an interview, head shot, networking event, etc. Please add your contact info if you would like to participate, and thank you to all of our BOW members for lending! Click here to view the clothing closet.
