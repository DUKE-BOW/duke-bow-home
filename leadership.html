BOW Leadership

BOW creates an environment where its members can thrive.

In our community, our members are given tools to develop as leaders, businesswomen, and individuals. Each year, several of our members serve on BOW's Executive Board to plan workshops, invite guest speakers to campus, form corporate sponsorships, carry out logistics, and more. The Executive Board strives to create the ultimate experience for Duke women interested in business by creating programming across all industries and skill sets.

Natalie Shammas

President


Natalie Shammas is a senior from Greenville, North Carolina. She is pursuing a B.A. in Psychology with a minor in Sociology and a Marketing & Management Studies Certificate. Natalie joined BOW her freshman year, and she has previously served on BOW’s executive board as VP Events for Diversified Industries and Logistics Chair. During her time in BOW, Natalie has strived to increase the diversity of industries represented on BOW’s calendar. This summer, she will be interning in Google’s marketing department. She has spent her previous summers interning with the WNBA’s Atlanta Dream and studying arts entrepreneurship through Duke In Chicago. In addition to BOW, Natalie works as a student manager with the Duke Women’s Basketball team and is an intern at the Fuqua/Coach K Center On Leadership and Ethics. She is also a Resident Assistant in Kilgo Dorm. In her free time, Natalie loves to watch NBA basketball, take spin classes, and spend time with her family and friends.
