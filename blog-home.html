Spotlight on Kendall Murphy, T'13

March 25, 2018 | By Ekim Buyuk and Shruti Rao

"[Do] not put pressure on yourself to find the perfect first job. You will likely change your mind and switch roles, teams, perhaps even whole industries several times in your first few years.
